REDUCE POLLUTION

Drive your car less. ... Keep your car in good repair. ... Turn off your engine. ... Don't burn your garbage. ... Limit backyards fire in the city. ... Plant and care for trees. ... Switch to electric or hand-powered lawn equipment. ... Use less energy


We can stop environmental pollution by planting more trees and taking care of the existing ones. Reduce the usage of vehicles, reuse and recycle items, proper disposal of waste, saying no to polythene and maintaining a proper sanitation and cleanliness in our surroundings could also reduce pollution. The first way to reduce pollution is to practice the 3Rs concept namely reduce, reuse and recycle.
